Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description

Peanut butter jelly sandwiches are under-rated. These are easy to make and so delicious. Perfect for when you are on the go!

Ingredients

  • bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. Using butter knife, spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Using same knife, spread jelly on another slice of bread
  3. Put both slices together and enjoy!
