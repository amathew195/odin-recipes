Avocado Toast

Description

Looking for a quick, healthy, and delicious breakfast? I highly recommend avocado toast. It only takes about 10 min and is super filling!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 small avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine avocado with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Top toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture
  3. Sprinkle crushed red pepper flakes on top
